Potato Disease Classification using CNN
Potato Disease Classification:

Potato leaf classification using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) is a cutting-edge technology that uses deep learning skills to categorise and distinguish various potato leaf kinds based on their visual features. CNNs, a subset of artificial neural networks, are adept at processing and recognising picture patterns. The method begins with a large dataset of potato leaf photos that have been thoroughly labelled with their different classifications. The CNN model then goes through a sequence of convolutional, pooling, and activation layers to extract important features and patterns from these images. Convolutional layers use small filters to scan the input leaf images methodically, capturing various features such as vein patterns, textures, and leaf forms.
